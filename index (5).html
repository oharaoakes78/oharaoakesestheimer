01 — Profile

Field-aligned operations leadership

I lead 24/7 industrial operations that support critical infrastructure in highly regulated environments — the kind of systems where downtime, drift, or a missed reading has real consequences. Across refineries and analytical labs, my work has centered on the same core loop: keep production safe and reliable, respond decisively when conditions turn, and develop the people and processes that make consistency possible.

My background sits at the intersection of the plant floor and the control room. I’ve directed blending and production decisions against regulatory constraints and system limits, run incident response and root-cause analysis on live disruptions, coordinated maintenance and readiness across operations and logistics, and supervised union crews with clear accountability for safety and performance.

Most recently I’ve carried that operational fluency into AI — building ground-truth datasets and evaluation rubrics that teach large language models how real energy-sector workflows actually behave. It turns out that knowing how a plant runs is exactly what’s needed to judge whether an AI understands it.
